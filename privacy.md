# Privacy Policy — Bitrise Pocket

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

Bitrise Pocket ("the App") is an unofficial third-party iOS client for the Bitrise CI/CD service. This policy describes what data the App handles.

## Data the App stores on your device

- **Bitrise Personal Access Token (PAT)** — stored in the iOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system. Used solely to authenticate requests to `https://api.bitrise.io`. Never transmitted anywhere else.
- **User preferences** — display options, filters, and last-viewed app/build IDs. Stored in the App's private container.

Deleting the App removes all of the above.

## Data the App sends over the network

The App communicates only with Bitrise's official API at `https://api.bitrise.io` over HTTPS, using your PAT as the `Authorization` header. No analytics SDKs, no third-party trackers, no crash-reporting services, no advertising networks are integrated.

## Data the developer collects

**None.** The developer has no servers, no backend, and receives no telemetry from the App.

## Data Bitrise.io collects

Bitrise.io is operated by Bitrise Ltd. and has its own privacy policy at [bitrise.io/privacy-policy](https://bitrise.io/privacy-policy). Any data sent to Bitrise's API is governed by that policy.

## Children

The App is not directed to children under 13.
